Escape of the Head Eater

Check out the Itch Page!

Developed in Unity/C#.

"Escape of the Head Eater" is a top-down shooter, where you play as a head that can eat other heads and attach itself to their bodies. Use the bodies to fight your way out of an evil science lab!

This game was made in a week!
